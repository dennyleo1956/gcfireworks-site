About G&C Fireworks

A Family-Owned, Veteran-Led Tradition

At G&C Fireworks, family, community, and celebration are at the heart of everything we do. Though we're from Arkansas and Tennessee and have lived all over the state, Northwest Arkansas is home — with our family rooted throughout the valley and NWA.

Outside of fireworks, we serve our community through healthcare at Mercy, WRMC, and Snell Prosthetics & Orthotics. But we wanted to create something special together that reflects our love for this community and the values of our country — and that vision became G&C Fireworks.

Greg is a veteran, and with military service running deep in both of our families, patriotism is in our blood. To us, fireworks are more than entertainment — they're a way to bring people together, honor traditions, and celebrate freedom.

We're proud to offer one of the best selections of fireworks in Fayetteville. Whether it's the Fourth of July, a family gathering, or a moment worth lighting up — we're here to help make it unforgettable.
